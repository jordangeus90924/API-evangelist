I’m continuing my [research into the future of web APis][1], and after watching leading [Mobile Backend as a Service (MBaaS) provider Kinvey start building APIs into their platform][2], I think MBaaS providers show a lot of potential for providing developers with quick access to API resources.

To help me monitor the MBaaS market I spent some time rounding up as many of the providers as I could, and so far I’ve found 24 of them:
